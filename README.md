# Nuer Font Converter

This is a **Streamlit app** to convert text written in fake Nuer fonts to **standard Unicode Nuer letters**.

It supports:
- Copying and pasting fake-font text
- Uploading TXT, DOCX, or PDF files
- Preserving Bible references, 4+ digit numbers, and brackets
- Converting fake Nuer letters like ` → ä, 1 → a̱, 2 → ë, 5 → i̱, etc.
- Copying converted text to clipboard
- Downloading converted text as TXT or DOCX

## Why use it
Converting materials to **standard Unicode Nuer letters** ensures your text is **digitally searchable and compatible** with modern tools.

## How to use
1. Paste your fake-font text or upload a file.
2. Click **Generate Converted Text**.
3. Copy or download the converted text.
